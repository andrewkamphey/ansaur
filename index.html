FAQ

Common questions

Can I buy a one-time export without subscribing?

Yes. Export Questions is $20: one CSV with up to 200 question gaps, volume data, and rank status. No recurring charge.

Do you use AI to generate keywords?

No. Every keyword comes from what people actually type into Google, with real search volume. AI may help format exports later; it will never invent opportunities.

What is a credit?

Credits are what you spend when you run research in Ansaur: checking ranks, discovering questions, filling search volume, and similar actions. Not everything costs credits, but many do. Your plan includes a monthly allowance, and you will see the cost before you run something.

Do I need API keys or YouTube login?

No. You should not need API keys or a Google Cloud console. Give us your niche topic and URLs. No OAuth, no account access required.

Can I use this for a blog, not YouTube?

Yes. Point it at your domain instead of a channel. Track published articles the same way you track videos.

I already have a keyword list. Can I upload it?

Yes. Upload your CSV and get volume plus gap status. No need to re-hunt phrases you already found.
