FAQ

Common questions

Can I buy a one-time export without subscribing?

Yes. Export Questions is $20: one CSV with up to 200 questions you have not answered yet, monthly search counts, and whether you already rank for each. No recurring charge.

Do you use AI to generate questions?

No. Every question comes from what people actually type into Google, with real monthly search counts. Ansaur never invents opportunities. We do use AI in Optimize Lab to help you improve articles you have already written (TL;DR blocks, FAQs, internal links, and similar). Question research itself stays data-driven.

What is a credit?

Credits are your monthly research allowance in Ansaur.com and you spend them on actions like seeing if you show up on Google, finding new questions with Scout, and filling in monthly search counts. You will see the cost before you run something.

Do I need API keys?

No. No login to your Google account required. Give us your niche topic and URLs. No API keys or YouTube login needed.

Can I use this for a blog?

Yes. Point it at your domain's blog. Track published articles. With future connections we can help you rank better.

Can I use this for YouTube?

Yes. Point it at your channel. Answer questions in video format. Track published videos ranking on Google Search. Views and audience will show up as "External" source in your channel's analytics.

I already have a list of questions. Can I upload it?

Yes. Upload your CSV and get monthly searches plus whether you already rank for each. No need to re-hunt phrases you already found.

Is this for kids?

It's for adults. It's for experts who want to share their expertise and experience. Yes it's a cute dinosaur mascot. My 5-year-old son is super curious and keeps asking soooooo many questions. And I just thought he would make the perfect mascot for this question finder. It makes me smile every time I work on this software, to see him here.

What is a top-up?

Sometimes you're deep in research and want to keep going even if you hit your monthly credits. A top up adds a temporary amount of credits to your account so you can keep going.
